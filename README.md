# Welcome to ApplikaDev 👋

**We build tools that help job seekers take control of their search — with clarity, data, and confidence.**

---

## What We're Building

Job hunting is exhausting. Dozens of applications scattered across spreadsheets, emails, and sticky notes. No way to know what's working and what isn't. No visibility into your own pipeline.

**[Applika.dev](https://applika.dev)** was built to fix that.

Applika is a free, open-source job application tracker that turns your job search into a structured, data-driven process — so you can focus on what matters: landing the right role.

---

## Features

- **Track every application** — Log company, role, platform, salary expectations, and all the details that matter, all in one place.

- **Follow your hiring pipeline** — Every application moves through its own step-by-step journey, from first contact to final answer.

- **See your conversion rates** — Understand at which stage you advance or get stuck, and how long each step takes on average.

- **Spot trends over time** — Visualize how many applications you're sending week by week and compare different sourcing strategies.

- **Close the loop** — Record final outcomes so every process — win or loss — becomes a learning opportunity.

- **Sign in with GitHub** — One click to get started. No forms, no extra passwords.

---

## Our Mission

We believe job seekers deserve better tools. Tools that are transparent, free to use, and that put the user — not the recruiter — in control.

Applika is fully open source. Every line of code is public. You can read it, use it, suggest improvements, or build on top of it. We think the best tools are built in the open, with the community they serve.
